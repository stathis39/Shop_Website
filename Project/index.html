RULES

In Quarto, there are 16 unique pieces, each of which is either:

  • Tall or short
  • Black or white
  • Square or circular
  • Hollow-top or solid-top

A player wins by doing a QUARTO.

A QUARTO can be done by placing on the board four pieces with a common attribute in a line or in a row.

At the beginning of his turn, a player places on the board the piece selected by his opponent.

If a QUARTO is done, the current player won. If not, he selects a piece for his opponent, and his turn is over.

The first player to do a QUARTO wins the game!
